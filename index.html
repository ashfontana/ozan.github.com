Hi, my name is Ozan Onay.

I'm a software developer and programmer based in San Francisco.

Being a programmer means speaking to computers in languages we both understand (for me this is Python, Ruby and JavaScript). Being a software developer means programming in a way that is effective, maintainable and useful for humans, usually alongside other software developers.

I practice my craft as co-founder and VP of engineering at Topguest. I occasionally blog here about writing code, and on Regarding Work about work practices, productivity and collaboration.

To get in touch, you can email me, follow me on twitter, or connect with me on linkedin.
